# 🏛️ Lab 10 — Final capstone project

> **Final mission:** demonstrate the complete DevOps cycle. **Reward:** +200 XP and the 🏛️ *DevOps Architect* badge.

## The challenge

In **teams** (or solo), demonstrate in ~5 minutes the complete workflow you built
throughout the course, using the **DevOps Academy App** (or your own variant).

## Demo checklist

1. [ ] **Repository** versioned with git.
2. [ ] **Application** running locally.
3. [ ] **Dockerfile** that builds the image.
4. [ ] **Docker Compose** bringing up app + database.
5. [ ] **CI/CD pipeline** green.
6. [ ] **Docker image** built.
7. [ ] **Kubernetes manifests** applied.
8. [ ] **Deployment** running in the cluster.
9. [ ] **Service** exposing the app (port-forward).
10. [ ] **Scaling** to multiple replicas.
11. [ ] **Logs** inspected.
12. [ ] **Image scan** (Trivy).
13. [ ] **Basic monitoring** (or an explanation of monitoring).
14. [ ] **Teaching proposal:** how to adapt it to YOUR subject.

## The layer that makes this course different (Layer 4: teaching application)

It's not enough that it works. In your submission, answer:

- How would you **explain** it to your students?
- Which part works as a **gradable exercise**?
- What common **mistakes** will show up, and how do you recover from them?
- What **learning outcome** are you trying to measure?
- What would you **change** for a specific subject (Programming, Databases, Networking, Security...)?

## Submission

Use the [submission template](plantilla-entrega.en.md) and review the [rubric](rubrica.en.md) before presenting.

| Subject | Lab focus |
